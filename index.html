  • HOW TO PLAY WORDVENTURE

    Instructions

    1. Guess the secret word in given chances.
    2. The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
    3. After incorrect guesses, the colors of the tiles will flip to gray.
    4. Each letter that matches the secret word but is in the wrong position will be marked in a yellow spot.
    5. Each letter that matches the secret word and is in the correct position will be marked in a green spot.
